PRE REQUISITES OF AUTOMATION X
  • 1. Account activation – For activation of account and resetting of password on the e-Invoice portal, it is mandatory to provide email IDs of suppliers/service providers in TML vendor master. If the vendor master is not updated with all the email IDs, please get in touch with the Purchase department of TML to ensure the same is updated.
  • 2. PO No.- Each invoice should contain the PO Number issued by TML.
  • 3. Other details in Invoice – Invoice should contain all the data elements as per GST Act specification i.e. Invoice No (should not be more than 15 digits), Invoice Date, GST No. etc
  • 4. Invoice should be printed and legible – Handwritten invoice will be treated as invalid. All Invoices should be computer printed using a printer that generates a clearly legible print. This has a direct bearing on OCR.
  • 5. No. of pages for Invoice - Invoice should not be more than one page long.
  • 6. One Invoice for One PO - Separate Invoices should be raised for each PO. A single invoice cannot be raised for multiple POs.
  • 7. Submission of invoice through Portal - Supplier/ Service provider has to select the PO data on the portal and upload the soft copy of the invoice through the portal.
  • 8. Invoice data should match with PO data - In case of any mismatch between uploaded invoice and PO data selected, Supplier/ Service provider will not be able to submit the invoice. In other words, all PO data like Rate, HSN Code, PO No, GSTN No, etc. should match with the data mentioned in the Invoice. In case of any discrepancies, the portal will not accept the invoice for submission.
  • 9. Restrictions – Please do not underline PO number, GST No, Invoice No, invoice date etc. in the invoice.
